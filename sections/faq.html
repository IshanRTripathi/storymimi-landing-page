Frequently Asked Questions

What is StoryMimi?

StoryMimi is an AI-powered app that creates personalized bedtime stories for children. You can customize characters, plot points, and even narrate stories in your own voice.

How does the AI personalization work?

Our advanced AI uses your input to generate unique story narratives and even create custom illustrations that feature your child as the main character. You can also clone your voice for narration.

Is StoryMimi suitable for children with disabilities?

Yes! StoryMimi is designed with inclusivity in mind. We offer features like dyslexia-friendly fonts, color blindness filters, and high-contrast modes to ensure every child can enjoy our stories.

What platforms will StoryMimi be available on?

StoryMimi will be available on iOS and Android devices. Join our waitlist to be notified upon launch!

How much does StoryMimi cost?

We offer a free trial, a monthly Family Plan, and a special Educator Plan. Please refer to our pricing section for more details.
